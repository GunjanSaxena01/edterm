A company implements a CI/CD pipeline where developer push code to git, jenkins builds a 
docker image, pushes it to docker hub, and deployes it to a swarm cluster. however, 
during peak development cycles, pipeline execution becomes slow and deployments overlap,
causing unstable releases. Analyze bottlenecks in jenkins archietecture, docker build 
concurrency, and registry limitations.Propose a scalable and optimised enterprise-grade 
CI/CD architecture.
